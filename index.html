Interview Notes - Women's Dressing Fashion Topics

The weather wasn't exactly warm, but Wang was dressed coolly, which made me instantly recognize her on the street for the interview!
"Sunglasses are a fashion item I can't handle. I would feel like a blind masseuse with sunglasses on. " jokes Wang, 23. The evolution of her personal style of dressing began in sixth grade, when she was she first started buying her own clothes and trying to change her hair and dress. Although her parents didn't quite understand her preferred style of dressing, even emphasizing the aesthetics of traditional women's clothing, she didn't let it influence her too much and was brave enough to explore the diverse beauty of the clothing design industry. When Wang was asked about the influence of gender norms on her fashion choices, she shook her head and dismissed the idea: "Rebellion is in my nature. Tell me what to do and I will do the opposite. No one dictates exactly what a woman should wear." When I complimented Wang on her dress appeal and asked her how to exude personal charm, she happily shared her feelings, "Be true to yourself, as cheesy as that sounds. Fully understanding the style that suits you and embracing it will be what keeps you on the positive path." Later on, when the topic of appearance anxiety came up, Wang's opinion was clear: "Anxiety? That's for having to conform to the standards of mass society and feeling pressured because of the fear of not conforming to the mainstream. Thousands of people have thousands of faces, I think the main thing is to follow the rhythm of their favorite way to do well, for example, I wear their favorite clothes. Xiao Wang is full of confidence, she will be brave to be herself, for her, what she wears on her body is not just clothes - it is her armor to face the world fearlessly.
